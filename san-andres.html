San Andres Island

San Andrés is a small Colombian island off Nicaragua in the Caribbean. It’s known for its white-sand beaches, coconut palms, clear water and all-inclusive resorts.

Part of an archipelago that includes Providencia and Santa Catalina, the island has a culture blending Latin American, English and Creole influences.

It's also home to expansive coral reefs and diverse marine life.
